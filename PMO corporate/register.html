All investing is subject to risk, including the possible loss of the money you invest. Past performance is not a guarantee or reliable indicator of future results. Unit prices, yields, and investment returns will fluctuate, and you may have a gain or loss when you redeem your units. Diversification does not ensure a profit or protect against a loss in a declining market.

Investors should carefully consider the investment objectives, risks, charges, and expenses of a fund before investing.
