EOP Portal Specifications & Guidelines

Understand the system architecture, security gates, and requirements.

Security & Integrity
  • SSM Integration: Live credentials authentication prevents corporate spoofing.
  • Bank-Level Mandate: Requires digital or physical authorization board resolution.
  • Frictionless Direct Debit: Secure tokens Authorise automatic monthly matching deductions.
  • Maker-Checker Isolation: Separates preparations from authorizations to eliminate rogue activities.
Setup Prerequisites
  1. Company SSM Registration Certificate (Form 9/Form 24).
  2. EPF Employer Registration Reference ID.
  3. Approved Bank Direct Debit authorization form.
Compliance & Auditing

All payroll modifications, contributions, and employee updates processed via EOP are strictly matched against Bank Negara Malaysia compliance directives and EPF e-contribution protocols. Secure, downloadable PDFs are generated for audit reporting.
