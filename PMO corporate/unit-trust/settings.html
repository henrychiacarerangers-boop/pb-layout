Password Requirements:
  • Mixture of both upper and lower case letters
  • Numeric characters (e.g. 1,2,3)
  • Between 8 and 16 characters without spaces
  • Special characters (~!@$%^*_=+[]|;:./?)

Practising Safe and Secure Password

  • Use a combination of numeric, special characters, upper and lower case letters. (e.g. Smrt1zv3@).
  • Password is case sensitive, e.g. "APPLE123" is different from "apple123".
  • Avoid using dictionary or generic passwords or a password that is easily identified with you (e.g. pass1234, birthday etc).
  • Create a password that is not easy to guess but easy to remember.
  • Do not share your password or write it down. Be wary of unexpected calls or emails requesting for personal information, passwords or financial details. Public Mutual will never send you an email or SMS asking you to verify or provide personal/confidential information.
  • Choose a different password for each online account. For example, using the same password for bank accounts and social media or email may increase the risk of identity theft or fraud.
  • If you suspect that your password has been compromised, call Customer Service Hotline immediately at 03-2022 5000.
