Important Investment & Risk Information

All investing is subject to risk, including the possible loss of the money you invest. Past performance is not a guarantee or reliable indicator of future results. Unit prices, yields, and investment returns will fluctuate, and you may have a gain or loss when you redeem your units. Diversification does not ensure a profit or protect against a loss in a declining market.

Investors should carefully consider the investment objectives, risks, charges, and expenses of a fund before investing. The prospectus, master prospectus, and Product Highlights Sheet (PHS) contain this and other key information. You can obtain copies of these documents from any Public Mutual branch, Authorised Unit Trust Consultant (UTC), or online. Please read them thoroughly before making any investment decisions.

Regulatory & Licensing Disclosures

Public Mutual Berhad 197501001842 (23419-A) is a registered unit trust management company under the Capital Markets and Services Act 2007, regulated and supervised by the Securities Commission Malaysia (SC). Public Mutual is also a registered Institutional Private Retirement Scheme Adviser (IPRSA) and a member of the Federation of Investment Managers Malaysia (FIMM).

For Shariah-compliant funds, investments are managed in strict adherence to Shariah principles under the supervision and guidance of our appointed Shariah Advisers. Private Retirement Schemes (PRS) are subject to retirement savings regulations, and early withdrawals from sub-account B may be subject to tax penalties and statutory restrictions.

Public Mutual Online (PMO), PMO Corporate, and UTC Connect are proprietary digital platforms operated by Public Mutual Berhad. By accessing these portals, you agree to be bound by the Terms and Conditions of Use, Website Disclaimer, and Privacy Policy. Electronic transactions are secured with industry-standard encryption protocols. Safeguard your login credentials; Public Mutual will never ask for your password or PAC codes via email or SMS.
